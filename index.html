Fueling Projects, One Community at a Time.

Empower your favorite Pump.fun tokens with DexVault. Anyone can start a campaign by submitting a token's contract address. DexVault automatically pulls live token data and generates a secure escrow wallet to raise community funds. You can choose between different campaign types. Each campaign has a customizable duration from 12-24 hours (up to 36 hours for premium boosts) to reach its funding goal. Any extra funds contributed beyond the campaign goal will be considered a tip and used to support platform maintenance and future improvements.
